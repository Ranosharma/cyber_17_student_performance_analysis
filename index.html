How Performance is Calculated

Our performance analysis system uses a comprehensive approach to evaluate student performance:

  • Academic Performance (70% weight): Based on average marks across all subjects
  • Attendance (20% weight): Regular attendance is crucial for academic success
  • Subject Feedback (10% weight): Student feedback and engagement with subjects

The final performance score is calculated as:

Performance Score = (Average Marks × 0.7) + (Attendance Percentage × 0.2) + (Average Feedback Rating × 0.1)

Performance Categories:

  • Excellent (90-100): Outstanding performance across all metrics
  • Good (75-89): Above average performance with room for improvement
  • Average (60-74): Satisfactory performance but needs attention
  • Poor (Below 60): Requires immediate intervention and support
